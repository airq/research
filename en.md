## Biography



Dr. Qinan Han is currently working as an Associate Professor at *Nanjing University of Aero. and Astro. (NUAA)*. He received his bachelor's degree (BEng) from *Tsinghua University* in 2013. He obtained his Ph.D from *Tsinghua University* in 2018 under the supervision of Prof. Huiji Shi.


**Research interests:**
- Fatigue and fretting fatigue of Ni-based superalloys
- Characterization of advanced alloys using in-situ SEM and EBSD
- Crystal plasticity simulation considering grain boundary cracking of single-crystal and poly-crystal alloys
- Data-driven method to model material properties
